Art in the Philippines

The works of art that have developed and accumulated in the Philippines from the beginning of civilization in the country up to the present era.

It reflects to its society and non-Filipinos the wide range of cultural influences on the country's culture and how these influences honed the country's arts. The art of the Philippines can be divided into two distinct branches, namely, traditional arts, and non-traditional arts.

Visual Arts of the Philippines

Including, but not limited to painting, non-folk sculpture, printmaking, photography, installation art, mixed media works, illustration, graphic arts, performance art, and imaging.
